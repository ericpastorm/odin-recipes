Cod with ratatouille

Its a beautiful, simple and healthy dish. Combines a tasteful fish like cod with a full bowl of vegetables and tomato sauce.

Ingredients:

  • Cod - 200g per person
  • Ratatouille - 100g per person
  • Flour

Steps:

  1. Batter the cod with flour.
  2. Put oil in a frypan to fry the cod.
  3. When the cod are near to finish, in other frypan put olive oil and heat at medium power for a minute.
  4. Put the ratatouille in the frypan with olive oil, wait a couple of minutes and add the cod, remove for a one minute.
  5. Eat! :)
